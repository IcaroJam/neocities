TROIKA!

A wild and imaginative ttrpg based on the foundations of chaos itself.


When I first talked my friends into trying the game out, I got struck by the hyper-focusing lighting, and decided I had to make custom stuff for them. I couldn't really find any nice looking character sheets in Spanish, so I went and designed my own. Inkscape is truly something let me tell you.

Now, I'm no designer, but I think it turned out nice enough to place it here so anyone can use it if they want!
